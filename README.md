A Vue.js wellness awareness website featuring guided breathing exercises, wellness tips, and AI-powered supportive messages.

- Home – Welcome & overview
- Breathing – Interactive 4-7-8 breathing exercise with animated timer
- Wellness – Mental health tips & resources (in progress)
- AI Support – AI chatbot for emotional support (in progress)
